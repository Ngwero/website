Help Center

Find answers to your questions and get the support you need

FOR SUBSCRIBERS (CUSTOMERS)

What is RukaPay and how do I use it?

+

RukaPay is a convenient mobile platform that lets you pay for goods and services, and transfer money, instantly. You can pay using QR codes, our simple USSD service (*289#), or the RukaPay mobile app.

Do I need an account to use RukaPay?

+

Yes, an account is required to unlock all the benefits RukaPay has to offer, including access to your transaction history.

How secure is RukaPay?

+

Yes, Extremely secure. We use bank-grade encryption, multi-layer authentication, and real-time transaction alerts to guarantee your safety and privacy with every payment

Are there any charges when I make payments?

+

Payments made directly to merchants are free of charge. Our in-app and USSD money transfer services have a minimal transaction fee.

How do I know if my payment was successful?

+

You'll receive an SMS immediately after a successful payment.

What should I do if a transaction fails or I'm charged wrongly?

+

Please contact RukaPay support right away with your transaction ID and the time it occurred. We aim to resolve most issues within 24 hours.

Can I use RukaPay without internet access?

+

Yes. Yes! You can easily access our services using the *USSD code *289# on any mobile phone, no internet required.

What should I do if my account top-up doesn't show?

+

First, log out and log back in to your account, and check your account statement to see if the top-up has posted. If it still hasn't reflected, please gather the Transaction ID and time of the transaction and contact our Customer Support team.

Is there a way to track my past payments?

+

Yes. If you have an account, you can view your transaction history on the RukaPay App.

I didn't receive my One-Time Password (OTP). What should I do?

+

Please wait at least one minute. Then, check your Spam/Junk folder, and finally, ensure the phone number your entering is correct

I forgot my PIN. How do I recover it?

+

Simply use the "Forgot PIN" link on the login screen of the app. Follow the secure, automated recovery steps which will involve answering your Security Questions.

FOR MERCHANTS

What is RukaPay and how can it help my business?

+

RukaPay is a digital payment platform that allows you to accept payments securely through QR codes, USSD, POS, or mobile apps. It helps you manage transactions, get reports, and expand your payment options for customers.

How do I register my business on RukaPay?

+

To register, visit the RukaPay Offices at TMT building 2nd Floor, Naguru-Bukoto, Kira Rd, Kampala. Alternatively, a RukaPay field agent can visit your location to help with the onboarding process.

What documents do I need to register as a merchant?

+

You'll need a valid business registration certificate,a nationalID, and an active bank or mobile money account.

How do I receive payments from customers?

+

Customers can easily pay you by scanning your QR code, dialing your USSD code, or through your POS terminal (if applicable). You'll receive an instant, confirmed notification for every successful payment.

How do I access my transaction history?

+

You can view your transaction history via the RukaPay merchant dashboard or app.You'll also receive daily summaries by email or SMS.

Are there any transaction fees?

+

Yes, minimal transaction fees apply depending on the payment method used. You can view the full tariff breakdown in your merchant portal.

How do I get a RukaPay QR code or POS device?

+

Once you're approved, RukaPay provides a free digital QR code. For POS devices, you can request one via your dashboard or agent (a deposit may apply).

Can I get support if I have issues with payments?

+

Yes our merchant support teams available 24/7.You can reach us via the in-appchat, email, or by calling 0200244410

How do I reconcile my daily transactions?

+

Use the dashboard's reconciliation feature or download daily/weekly statements to compare with your internal records.

Can I add multiple branches to my account?

+

Absolutely. You can manage multiple branches from your single main account

FOR AGENTS

Who is a RukaPay agent?

+

A RukaPay Agent is our local representative, serving as a crucial link between RukaPay and the community. You help users and merchants with registration, cash deposits, withdrawals, and minor issue resolution.

How can I become a RukaPay agent?

+

To apply, visit our offices at TMT Building 2nd Floor, Naguru-Bukoto, Kira Rd, Kampala. Please bring your ID, a passport photo, and proof of address. Approval usually takes 48-72 hours.

What services can I offer as an agent?

+

You can provide a range of essential services, including: Customer registration, Cash-in and cash-out (deposits/withdrawals), Merchant onboarding, Float management, and Customer support.

Do I need capital to start?

+

Yes, you need initial float minimum UGX 300,000 to process withdrawals and deposits.

How much commission do I earn?

+

Agents earn a percentage-based commission on every single transaction processed. You can find the detailed commission structure within your dedicated agent portal.

What support does RukaPay provide agents?

+

We provide branded materials, training, a digital dashboard, and a dedicated agent support line.

How do I manage my float?

+

You can transfer float via mobile money or bank deposit. Your dashboard lets you monitor balances in real time.

What should I do in case of fraud or suspicious activity?

+

Immediately report any suspicious transactions through the agent hotline. Protecting you, your customers, and your float is our highest priority.

Can I have more than one agent outlet?

+

Yes. You can expand to more locations. Each will be registered under your master account.

What happens if I lose my phone or access to my account?

+

Contact our support team immediately so we can suspend your account and help you regain secure access without delay.

How to Delete Your RukaPay Account

+
Warning: Deleting your account is irreversible. All your data will be permanently removed.
  1. Log In: Open the RukaPay app and log in with your credentials.
  2. Go to Settings: Tap the settings icon (⚙️) in the top-left, then select Settings.
  3. Account & Security: Within Settings, tap Account & Security.
  4. Delete Account: Scroll down and tap Delete Account. A confirmation screen appears.
  5. Confirm Deletion: Enter your pin and tap Confirm Delete. Your account and all data will be permanently removed.

Need help? Contact our support team.

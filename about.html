About RukaPay

Secure, simple payments for everyone in Uganda.

Rhino Payment Services Ltd (trading as RukaPay) is a Bank of Ugandalicensed PSP/PSO on a mission to make money movement easier for people, SACCOs, and businessesonline, instore, and everywhere in between.

Why we exist

Our Mission

Simple Secure Everywhere

Make payments effortless

We reduce friction across collections, transfers and payoutsso Ugandans can move money confidently, online and offline.

  • One QR for MTN & Airtel collections
  • App, USSD *289# and NFC options
  • Nextday settlements to bank or mobile money

Trust by design

Security, compliance and reliability are built into every product decisionnot added later.

  • Licensed PSP/PSO (Bank of Uganda)
  • Bankgrade encryption & monitoring
  • Fraud controls and audit trails

Payment Channels & Services

RukaPay enables fast, secure payments for individuals, SACCOs, and businesses through unified channels built for modern financial needs.

  • Scan to Pay (QR) MTN & Airtel
  • Tap to Pay (NFC)
  • USSD (*289#)
  • Wallet-to-Wallet transfers
  • Merchant Collections
  • Bulk Payouts & Transfers

How RukaPay Works

Simple, secure, and smart payment solutions for everyone

Scan to Pay

1

Open RukaPay app and select "Scan to Pay"

2

Point camera at merchant's QR code

3

Enter amount and confirm payment

4

Transaction complete! Receipt sent instantly

Tap to Pay

1

Enable NFC on your smartphone

2

Open RukaPay app and select "Tap to Pay"

3

Tap phone on merchant's NFC terminal

4

Confirm with PIN or biometric authentication

APP TO APP

1

Open RukaPay app on your smartphone

2

Navigate to "Send Money" or "Payments"

3

Enter recipient phone number and amount

4

Confirm with PIN or biometric to complete

USSD Service

1

Dial *289# on any phone

2

Select service from menu

3

Enter recipient details and amount

4

Confirm with PIN to complete transaction

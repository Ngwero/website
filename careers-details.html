CHIEF BUSINESS DEVELOPMENT OFFICER

Full Time
Kampala, Uganda
Deadline: Friday, 22nd August 2025

Job Description

We are seeking a dynamic and experienced Chief Business Development Officer to lead our business development initiatives and drive growth for RukaPay. This role will be responsible for developing and executing strategic partnerships, expanding our market presence, and driving revenue growth in Uganda's rapidly evolving fintech landscape.

Key Responsibilities

  • Develop and execute comprehensive business development strategies to drive growth and market expansion
  • Identify and cultivate strategic partnerships with banks, telecom companies, and other key stakeholders
  • Lead negotiations for major business deals and partnership agreements
  • Build and maintain relationships with key industry players and regulatory bodies
  • Analyze market trends and competitive landscape to identify new opportunities
  • Collaborate with cross-functional teams to ensure successful implementation of business initiatives
  • Represent RukaPay at industry events, conferences, and stakeholder meetings
  • Develop and manage the business development budget and performance metrics

Qualifications

  • Bachelor's degree in Business, Finance, IT or related field (MBA preferred)
  • Minimum of 5 years' experience in senior leadership roles within fintech, banking, or telecom
  • Proven track record of successfully scaling business operations and partnerships
  • Strong understanding of the Ugandan financial services market and regulatory environment
  • Excellent strategic thinking and analytical skills

Required Skills

  • Exceptional negotiation and stakeholder engagement skills
  • Strong strategic planning and commercial acumen
  • Proven leadership capabilities with experience managing teams
  • Excellent communication and presentation skills
  • Ability to work in a fast-paced, dynamic environment
  • Strong network within the Ugandan business community
  • Proficiency in financial modeling and business case development

What We Offer

  • Competitive salary and benefits package
  • Opportunity to shape the future of digital payments in Uganda
  • Dynamic and innovative work environment
  • Professional development and growth opportunities
  • Work with cutting-edge technology and industry experts
  • Make a meaningful impact on financial inclusion in Uganda

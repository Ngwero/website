Important terms you need to understand when reading this notice? ▶

  • Data Subject: An identified or identifiable natural person. An identifiable natural person is one who can be identified, directly or indirectly, by reference to an identifier or one or more factors specific to their physical, physiological, genetic, mental, economic, cultural, or social identity. This term also encompasses any additional persons granted data privacy rights and protection of Personal Data under applicable data privacy laws.
  • Local Regulatory Requirements: Legal, statutory, regulatory, licensing conditions, rules, guidelines, ministerial or national security orders or directives, and public-safety or data-sovereignty–related requirements with which RukaPay is required to comply in the jurisdictions where we operate.
    Data sovereignty refers to the laws and governance structures that apply to Personal Data based on the geographic location where it is processed.
  • Personal Data: Any information relating to a Data Subject from which that person can be identified. Examples include, but are not limited to:
    • Name, date of birth, nationality, marital status
    • Educational level or occupation
    • Identifying numbers or symbols (e.g., national ID, customer number)
    • Contact information (e.g., email address, postal address, telephone number)
    • Location data or physical address
    • Online identifiers or other unique assignments
    • Biometric information (e.g., fingerprint, facial recognition data)
    • Personal opinions, views, or preferences
    • Correspondence of a private or confidential nature
    • Opinions of others about the Data Subject
    • Any combination of data that, when linked to a name, reveals further personal information
    • Sensitive Personal Data (see below)
  • Processing: Any operation or set of operations performed on Personal Data—whether by automated means or not—including: collection, receipt, recording, organization, structuring, collation, storage; adaptation or alteration; retrieval, consultation, use; dissemination or disclosure by transmission; alignment or combination; restriction, erasure, destruction, or degradation.
  • Sensitive Personal Data: A subset of Personal Data deemed more sensitive and deserving of additional protection. This includes, but is not limited to:
    • Racial or ethnic origin
    • Political opinions or affiliations
    • Religious or philosophical beliefs
    • Trade-union membership
    • Criminal history or proceedings
    • Genetic or biometric data
    • Health-related information
    • Data concerning sex life or sexual orientation

Is RukaPay allowed to process my Personal Data? ▶

We are permitted to process your Personal Data only when we have a valid legal basis under applicable law or our internal policies. For each Processing activity, RukaPay will rely on one of the following bases as appropriate:

  • Performance of a Contract
    Processing is necessary to enter into or fulfill our contractual obligations with you (e.g., maintaining your RukaPay account, executing your payment instructions).
  • Legal Obligation
    Processing is required for RukaPay to comply with a legal or regulatory duty (e.g., "know your customer" (KYC) requirements, anti–money laundering rules).
  • Legitimate Interests
    Processing is in RukaPay's—or a third party's—legitimate interest (such as fraud prevention, system security, or service enhancement), provided those interests do not override your fundamental rights and freedoms.
  • Vital Interests
    Processing is necessary to protect the life or physical safety of you or another person (e.g., in emergency situations involving your account).
  • Public Interest
    Processing is necessary to carry out a task performed in the public interest or in the exercise of official authority vested in RukaPay (where applicable).
  • Consent
    Where none of the above apply (or where required by law), we will seek your explicit consent before Processing certain categories of your Personal Data. You may withdraw consent at any time without affecting the lawfulness of any Processing carried out before withdrawal.

When Processing Sensitive Personal Data, we will rely on one of the following, as applicable:

  • Explicit Consent
    You have clearly agreed to the specific use of your Sensitive Personal Data and may withdraw this consent at any time.
  • Vital Interests
    Processing is necessary to protect your life or the life of another person, and you are unable—physically or legally—to give consent.
  • Public Disclosure by You
    The Sensitive Personal Data has been intentionally made public by you.
  • Legal Claims and Judicial Proceedings
    Processing is necessary for establishing, exercising, or defending legal claims, or in the context of judicial proceedings.
  • Research & Statistics
    Processing is required for scientific, historical, or statistical purposes, provided that it is proportionate, respects data-protection rights, and incorporates appropriate safeguards.

Compliance with Local Law
Processing of any Personal Data—including data relating to criminal behavior—will always comply with the local regulatory requirements in the jurisdictions where RukaPay operates.

How does RukaPay secure my Personal Data? ▶

RukaPay protects the integrity and confidentiality of your Personal Data by implementing appropriate and reasonable technical, physical, and organizational safeguards designed to prevent:

  • Accidental loss, damage, or destruction of your Personal Data;
  • Unauthorized or unlawful access to your Personal Data; and
  • Unauthorized or unlawful processing of your Personal Data.

To achieve this, RukaPay regularly:

  • Identifies and assesses risks — we conduct periodic risk assessments of all foreseeable internal and external threats to Personal Data under our control.
  • Implements layered security controls — we deploy encryption, access controls, firewalls, intrusion detection systems, and secure development practices to harden our platforms and infrastructure.
  • Applies physical protections — our offices and data centers are secured with restricted access, surveillance, and environmental safeguards to prevent unauthorized entry or environmental damage.
  • Enforces organizational policies — we maintain strict data-handling policies, regular staff training, and incident-response procedures to ensure all team members understand and uphold our privacy and security standards.

These measures are reviewed and updated continuously to adapt to new threats and to maintain compliance with applicable data-protection regulations.

How does RukaPay handle Personal Data Breaches? ▶

While RukaPay implements reasonable measures to minimize the likelihood and impact of Personal Data breaches, no system can be entirely risk-free. If RukaPay becomes aware of—or reasonably suspects—a breach that compromises the integrity or confidentiality of Personal Data, we will follow our established Incident Response and Management procedures. These include containment, investigation, remediation, compliance reporting, and notification to affected individuals and regulators as required by law.

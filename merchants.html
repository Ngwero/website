How Bulk Payments Work

The streamlined RukaPay payroll process

1

Employee Registration

The Finance Manager registers employees with essential details:

  • Full name and identification number
  • Department and job title
  • Gross monthly salary
  • Preferred payment method (bank or mobile money)
  • Account or mobile wallet number
2

Payroll Generation

For each payment cycle, the Finance Manager:

  • Selects the applicable month
  • Inputs deductions:
    • Loan Repayments
    • Salary Advances
    • Other Deductions (penalties, taxes, etc.)
  • System calculates: Net Salary = Gross Salary - (Loan + Advance + Other Deductions)
  • Generates payroll with "Pending Review" status
3

Review & Verification

The Chief Finance Officer (CFO):

  • Reviews all payroll entries
  • Verifies financial accuracy and policy compliance
  • Coordinates corrections with Finance Manager if needed
  • Marks payroll as "Checked"
4

Executive Approval

The Chief Executive Officer (CEO):

  • Performs final approval of payroll
  • Confirms compliance with strategic requirements
  • Updates status to "Ready for Payment"
5

Disbursement

The Finance Manager:

  • Initiates disbursement of approved payroll
  • System automatically processes payments via:
    • Bank transfer
    • Mobile money (RukaPay, Airtel)
  • Status updated to "Paid"
  • Disbursement report generated
